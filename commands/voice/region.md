# region

#### Command Examples:

```fix
/region <region>
```

#### Arguments:

- **region** - The region to set the voice channel

#### Description:

Changes the region of your voice channel, the valid regions are:

- Brazil
- Hong Kong
- India
- Japan
- Rotterdam
- Russia
- Singapore
- South Africa
- Sydney
- US Central
- US East
- US South
- US West
